Who We Are

Built on regional expertise, run with modern systems

Masar Logistics was founded to close a gap in the Saudi freight market: international-grade reliability, delivered with the responsiveness of a local partner. Our team combines decades of hands-on freight, customs, and supply chain experience with the kind of technology — live tracking, digital documentation, route optimization — that used to be reserved for the largest global carriers.

Whether you're shipping a single container or managing a multi-site distribution network, we treat your cargo like it's the only shipment that matters.
